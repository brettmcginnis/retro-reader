# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 2026

## Overview

Retro Reader is a mobile application for reading retro game guides. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

The short version: it doesn't collect any.

## No Data Collection

Retro Reader does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no servers, no databases, and no mechanisms for gathering information about you or your usage.

## Local Storage Only

All app data — including bookmarks, pins, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using on-device storage (SQLite and AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## No Accounts or Login

The app does not have any authentication or account system. There is no sign-up, no login, and no user profiles.

## No Analytics or Tracking

Retro Reader does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. There are no usage metrics, crash reporters, or behavioral tracking tools of any kind.

## No Network Requests

The app does not communicate with external servers. It operates entirely offline using content stored on your device.

## Children's Privacy

Because Retro Reader collects no data whatsoever, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy under COPPA or similar regulations.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be made available alongside the app. The effective date at the top of this document will be updated accordingly.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at retroreaderapp@gmail.com.
